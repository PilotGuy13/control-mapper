# Acme Corp Information Security Policy (excerpt)

This sample policy is fictional and exists only to demonstrate the control mapper.
Each numbered statement is treated as a separate policy requirement during mapping.

1. All employees must complete security awareness training within 30 days of their
   start date and repeat the training every twelve months.

2. Access to production systems is granted on a least-privilege basis. Requests must
   be approved by the system owner, and access rights are reviewed every quarter.

3. Multi-factor authentication is required for all remote access to the corporate
   network and for any access to systems holding regulated data.

4. Passwords must be at least 14 characters, are rotated at least annually, and are
   stored only as salted hashes. Shared credentials are prohibited.

5. All customer data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and encrypted in transit
   using TLS 1.2 or higher.

6. Security-relevant logs from production systems are collected centrally, retained
   for at least one year, and reviewed weekly for anomalous activity.

7. Suspected security incidents must be reported to the security team within one
   hour of discovery. The team follows a documented incident response runbook.

8. Vulnerability scans of internet-facing systems are performed monthly, and
   critical findings are remediated within fifteen days.

9. The company maintains cyber liability insurance with coverage of at least
   five million dollars.

10. Backups of critical systems are taken daily, encrypted, and tested for
    restorability on a quarterly basis.
